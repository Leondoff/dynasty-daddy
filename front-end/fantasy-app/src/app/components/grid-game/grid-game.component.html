Additional Rules
You cannot use a player more than once. Once a player is locked in, they will be removed from the search results.
For NFL teams, our data has the team they finished the season on. For certain cases, players that were traded mid season after joining a new team might not be counted. I am working on covering that edge case.
For college teams, it is the college the player was draft from. If they transferred from one school to another it would be the school they transferred to.
For jersey numbers, any jersey number they wore on any team works. It doesn't have to be the jersey number they use on the grid team.
